FAQs

How long does a family petition take?

Timelines vary by category and country of origin. We’ll provide an estimate after reviewing your case and current processing times.

Can you help if I received an RFE or denial?

Yes. We analyze the notice, strengthen evidence, and respond or plan next steps, including motions or appeals where appropriate.

Do you offer payment plans?

We can discuss structured payments for many case types. Ask during your consultation.
