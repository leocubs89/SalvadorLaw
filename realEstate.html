Closing Process

1. Contract Review
We review deadlines, contingencies, and disclosures; propose addenda when needed.
2. Due Diligence
Coordinate inspection, appraisal, title, and survey; resolve issues before they become problems.
3. Clear to Close
Finalize lender conditions, prorations, closing statement, and coordinate your signing.
4. Closing Day
We attend the closing, review documents with you, and ensure keys and funds are transferred correctly.

FAQs

Do you offer flat fees for closings?

Yes, most standard residential closings are flat‑fee. We’ll confirm once we see your contract and property details.

What documents should I send first?

Executed contract, disclosures, lender contact, and any HOA information. We’ll provide a checklist after engagement.

Will you attend the closing?

Yes, an attorney from our office will attend (in person or remote where permitted) and review final documents with you.
