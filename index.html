About Our Firm

The Law Office of Lopez & Affiliates, Ltd., has been serving the Bensenville, Chicago and Elgin area since 1998. Our mission and philosophy is simple; we are a neighborhood law office dedicated to providing quality legal access to the community. We firmly believe that competent legal advocacy should not be a privilege for the few but a right for all. We want our communities to know that should it ever become necessary to seek out legal advice or assistance, do not hesitate to call us. If we cannot help you with your problem we will find someone who will and who shares in our philosophy.

Salvador Lopez Esq. is the founder of the law firm, which has guided the firm from day one. He received his B.A. in Philosophy in 1995 and his J.D. in 1998. Once licensed to practice law in Illinois, he quickly realized that the Latino community had grown much since he was a child and were badly advised in the aspect of legal representation. Then he expanded his practice from Elgin to Chicago, and then to Bensenville. Salvador’s firm has extensive experience in traffic, criminal, divorce, immigration and real estate cases. He has personally conducted many trials, obtained legal status for immigrants and has been involved in many real estate closings. He and his staff are bilingual and speak English and Spanish fluently. Salvador is licensed to practice law in Illinois and court of Northern District in Illinois.
