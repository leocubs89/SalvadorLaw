FAQs

How long does an uncontested divorce take?

Timelines vary by county and docket. With full agreement and complete documents, many cases can finalize in a few weeks.

Can parenting time be changed later?

Yes, courts may modify schedules based on significant changes in circumstances or best interests of the child.

Do you offer payment plans?

We can discuss structured payments depending on case type. Ask during your consultation.
