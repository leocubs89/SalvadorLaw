FAQs

Will I lose my license after a DUI arrest?

A statutory summary suspension may apply. You often have a short window to challenge it. We’ll review dates and options during your consultation.

Can a ticket be kept off my record?

In many cases, court supervision or amendments can prevent points and insurance hikes. Outcomes depend on your history and facts.

Do you handle felony charges?

Yes. We defend a range of felony matters and will assess discovery, motions, and trial posture to protect your rights.
